Additional Ideas

Looking for inspiration? Pick one of these for your Dealers Choice project.

  • "What's Happening to My Card" Explainer
    An interactive visual timeline that explains each step of the grading process to customers. Animated progress through: Receiving → ID/Entry → Grading → Encapsulation → QC → Shipping. Each step has a plain-language explanation.

    Why it matters: Reduces "where's my order" support tickets by educating customers
    Great for: First-time submitters
  • Card Show / Event Finder
    A map-based or list-based tool showing upcoming card shows, PSA submission events, and meet-and-greets. Filter by region, date, submission availability.

    Simple data display but customers constantly ask about this
  • AI Submission Form Reader
    Upload a photo of a submission form with handwritten notes → display the extracted fields in a clean editable table. Prototype the UI and flow (mock the AI extraction part, or wire up a vision API if ambitious).
  • Grading Floor "Spotify Wrapped"
    An end-of-year style recap for a grader or department: total cards graded, rarest card seen, busiest day, most common grade given, fun superlatives. Pure fun, but great for morale.
